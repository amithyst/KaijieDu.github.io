未来7年，我想寻找一条通往通用人工智能的快速途径。 我觉得，人脑其实就是不同的模型分工合作的结果。 复数个LLM负责“内部言语”(心理学概念)，一个LLM负责表达，一个“LLM”充当小脑和脑干传递神经信号。 人类自我意识的本质其实是能动性，能干涉外界的特性。谷歌近日提出了无限记忆的LLM架构。 当一个大语言模型通过与环境交互，能在无人与他交互的情况下探索，发现环境。无限制的生成“思绪”，自我意识也就诞生了。

如今的大语言模型依然处于“为人服务”的范式。用户发起提问，他回答，结尾，等待用户的反馈。 当大语言模型转变为“能依靠自我思索的无限生成-实践-反馈”模式时，AGI也就诞生了。 关于这种模式的训练方法，我已经有了一套不错的方案的初步想法。

STEP1 利用现有的大语言模型架构创造出能自我探索学习的AGI框架。 现有的研究工作如Infinite-LLM framework，StreamingLLM等都能一定程度上实现无限记忆；
STEP2 做一个多模态的输入端，能让LLM理解外界环境。
STEP3 做一个彻底自由的输出端，能让LLM彻底执行自己的想法。
STEP4 让LLM实现自我评估，设计奖励函数
STEP5 基于超真实虚拟环境(如minecraft等)，探索
STEP6 再将与训练好的模型让他“意识”到现实世界，增加具身智能等

I am Kaijie Du, an Applied Mathematics student at Fudan University’s School of Mathematical Sciences, with a profound interest in the research of reinforcement learning-based intelligent agents using large language models. My research ambition centers on developing AGI within realistic virtual environments like Minecraft, with a vision to eventually enable AGI to interact with and alter reality, enhancing embodied intelligence.

My academic journey is marked by a strong foundation in mathematics, showcased by my receipt of the Yongying Fund Scholarship and a first prize at the National Mathematics Competition for College Students in the Shanghai region. In addition to excelling in mathematics,I have explored the domains of artificial intelligence and programming, proficiently handling medium to easy level problems on LeetCode using Python, C++.

I have contributed to the field of computer vision through research integrating CLIP with domain knowledge for emotion recognition, culminating in a second-author paper submitted to CVPR, though it was not accepted in the latest cycle. My technical skills are further refined by familiarity with neural network frameworks like PyTorch and TensorFlow, extensive use of remote Linux servers, command-line interfaces, and the ability to replicate various projects from GitHub, leveraging tools like WSL and Docker for virtualization.

In the next seven years, I am looking to find a fast track to general artificial intelligence. I believe that the human brain is actually the result of different models working together. Multiple LLMs are responsible for "inner speech" (a psychological concept), one LLM is responsible for expression, and one "LLM" acts as the cerebellum and brainstem transmitting neural signals. The essence of human self-consciousness is actually agency, the characteristic that can interfere with the external world. Google recently proposed an LLM architecture with infinite memory. When a large language model interacts with the environment and is able to explore and discover the environment without anyone interacting with it, generating "thoughts" without limit, self-consciousness is born.

Today's large language models still operate within the paradigm of "serving humans." The user asks a question, it responds, ends, and waits for user feedback. When the large language model transitions to a mode of "infinite generation - practice - feedback" that can rely on self-thought, AGI will be born. I already have a preliminary idea of a good training method for this model.

STEP1 Use the existing large language model architecture to create an AGI framework that can explore and learn by itself. Current research works like the Infinite-LLM framework, StreamingLLM, etc., can all realize infinite memory to some extent.
STEP2 Make a multimodal input end that allows LLM to understand the external environment.
STEP3 Make a completely free output end that allows LLM to fully execute its own ideas.
STEP4 Let LLM implement self-assessment, design reward functions.
STEP5 Explore based on a hyper-realistic virtual environment (such as Minecraft, etc.).
STEP6 Then make the trained model "aware" of the real world, adding embodied intelligence, etc.
